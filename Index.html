About Me

Hey, I’m the artist behind MAZETATZ — a tattoo design brand rooted in creativity, intention, and collaboration. I believe tattoos aren’t just ink; they’re stories, emotions, and energy made visual. Every design I create is unique, just like the person wearing it. Whether you already know exactly what you want or you're still exploring, I’m here to help you shape your idea into a custom piece that fits you perfectly.

Services

  • Custom Tattoo Designs — Fully original, hand-drawn or digital artwork based on your vision.
  • Concept Development — Don’t have a clear idea yet? I’ll help you shape your thoughts into a tattooable design.
  • Flash Designs — Pre-made tattoos available to reserve or modify to your liking.

Note: I currently focus on design only, not tattooing. I collaborate with you to ensure your tattoo artist gets a clean, professional blueprint to work with.
